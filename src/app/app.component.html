ARCANE CHEATSHEET Standard Spell Areas: 9sq/45ft. Can be shaped any way, but all squares must touch at least one other square. Standard Concentration Length: 6 rounds/1 minute.
Using Energy: can spend 10 minutes to save once/day to detect and use a local energy source, making a single type of magic one magnitude cheaper.
Equipment
The character is allowed ten regular objects, plus five in a backpack, sack or similar container. Heavy objects count as two, while minor objects count as half. Coins, gems and very small things are no-size items—100 of them count as one regular object. Use common sense to settle what is what. Weapons have regular/heavy/minor designators. Armors, unless magickal, count as per AC
For every completed uneven number of regular objects exceeding the character’s limit, she moves down a category in the Movement Rates table. Strength can temporarily alleviate the consequences.

†RoF stands for Rate of Fire, measured in shots/round(s). This means that you fire in the first round and then need to spend any remaining rounds reloading before you can fire again. You can’t reload a weapon with a fraction RoF (crossbow, black powder pistol etc.) while moving.
§Reach means that enemies within 10 feet are within melee range. This is particularly important when dealing with free attcks.
