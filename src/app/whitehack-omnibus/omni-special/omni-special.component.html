general combat rules Initiative.  Roll a d6: high roll wins. If there is a tie, missiles before melee; lighter weapons before heavier ones.

  If you do nothing during your turn, you can change your initiative to 6 in following rounds.

  You can choose a lower position. In the first round after this choice, you can go before others on the same initiative.

 Actions During a Round.  attack→move→small action Free Attack & Disengaging  Casting a spell, quaffing a potion, taking something out of a bag, moving out of melee range, and similar actions give every ready enemy in melee range a free attack. You can only make one free attack per round, except for the Strong.

  To disengage, you must move backwards at a careful pace (-10ft/-2sq), still facing your opponent.

 Combat Advantage  The Referee decides when there is advantage, but generally flanking, high ground, sure footing vs. poor footing, back strikes or attacking unawares enemies grants +2AV/dmg. Persist. Once/combat, make an CON task roll or save against incoming damage.
  • On success, reduce dmg. by d6. This does not heal you.
  • On fail, take full dmg. If HP > 0, stunned for 2 rounds.
  • If dmg takes you into negative HP, die in d6 rounds.
